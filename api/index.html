Function reference

Painting local ancestry

The high-level entry point: fit the ancestry CTMC on the reference tips and paint the query haplotypes. paint returns a Painting; fit exposes the EM directly.

paint Infer soft local ancestry along query haplotypes from a tree sequence.
Painting Result of :func:paint: the soft local-ancestry posteriors plus the fitted model.
fit Blocked EM for (Q, π, {w_i}) (CLAUDE.md §3, §11.1.5-6).
FitResult Fitted parameters returned by :func:fit.

Output & segmentation

The soft per-haplotype posterior (Segment tracks) and its collapse into hard ancestry tracts for downstream tract-length / admixture-dating analysis.

output.posterior_table Per-sample ancestry posterior as contiguous segments covering [0, L).
output.hard_segments Collapse a soft posterior track into hard ancestry segments.
output.Segment A contiguous span carrying one soft ancestry posterior.
output.missing_info_mask Per-sample spans where the tree is uninformative (isolated sample).
output.posterior_at Posterior vector for sample at a genomic position.

Validation metrics

Accuracy, calibration, and fragmentation / tract-length metrics for simulated-truth experiments (the tspaint.metrics namespace).

validate.balanced_accuracy Mean of per-true-class painting accuracies.
validate.per_base_accuracy Span-weighted per-base painting accuracy.
validate.mean_confidence Span-weighted mean painter confidence \|2*P(state) - 1\|.
validate.reliability_curve Calibration curve of P(state): predicted vs. empirical per bin.
validate.breakpoint_precision_recall Precision/recall of inferred ancestry switch points vs. truth.
validate.switch_density Ancestry switches per unit length.
validate.tract_boundary_error Tract-boundary localization error against the truth.
validate.breakpoint_flicker Posterior discontinuity across a sample’s segment boundaries (CLAUDE.md §7.3).
validate.map_truth Remap truth tracts from population ids to ancestry-state indices.

Comparators (head-to-head)

Painters scored on equal footing by a uniform harness — the full method, a runnable ARG-native baseline, and the RFMix incumbent (the tspaint.compare namespace).

compare.tspaint_paint Paint queries with the full tspaint method (EM fit + down-pass posterior).
compare.nearest_reference_paint Paint queries by their nearest labelled reference (ARG-native baseline).
compare.rfmix_paint RFMix painter with the standard painter(ts, labels, queries) signature.
compare.head_to_head Score a panel of painters on one admixture scenario across ARG substrates.
compare.score_painter Run a painter on ts and score it against the truth.

Input front ends

Obtain a tspaint-ready tree sequence from genotypes (the tspaint.io namespace).

io.infer_tree_sequence Infer a tree sequence from the genotypes of ts_with_mutations via tsinfer.
io.add_mutations Overlay biallelic mutations on a tree sequence so tsinfer has variant data.
io.singer_tree_sequences Sample posterior ARGs for ts (which must carry mutations) via SINGER.
io.write_haploid_vcf Write one haploid VCF column per sample node.

Horizontal BP/EP smoother

The §7 horizontal coupling — a genome-axis forward-backward over each tip’s per-tree beliefs that suppresses spurious switches on inferred ARGs (the tspaint.bp namespace; also paint(smooth=True)).

bp.bp_paint tspaint painter with horizontal BP smoothing.
bp.bp_smooth Forward-backward over per-segment beliefs along the genome.
bp.bp_smooth_track Smooth one tip’s :class:~tspaint.output.Segment track along the genome.
bp.bp_vs_deadband_experiment Compare BP smoothing vs the deadband for segmentation, over seeds.

Admixture dating (rate through time)

An optional, separate deliverable from painting: a time-inhomogeneous directional mugration EM that estimates the cross-ancestry transition rates q_AB(t), q_BA(t) as functions of (backward) time — locating divergence / gene-flow epochs and their direction (the tspaint.dating namespace; also Painting.rate_through_time()).

fit_rate_through_time Fit the time-inhomogeneous directional admixture-rate-through-time profile by EM.

dating.make_Q_of_cell Build a cell_index -> (2,2) generator callable from per-cell directional rates.
dating.directional_rate_splines Fit q_AB(t) and q_BA(t) by GCV-selected penalised Poisson splines.
dating.fit_poisson_spline Penalised Poisson log-spline via penalised IRLS.
dating.select_lambda_gcv Fit at a grid of smoothing parameters; pick the one minimising GCV.
dating.log_time_grid Geometric (log-spaced) cell edges spanning [t_min, t_max].
dating.split_branch Split a branch [t_c, t_p] (t_c < t_p) into per-cell sub-intervals.
dating.branch_cell_stats Per-cell endpoint-conditioned expected dwell and directional jumps for one branch.

Simulation (known truth)

msprime admixture scenarios with census-event ground truth — the primary validation track and the source of examples.

simulate_admixture Simulate an admixed sample plus two reference panels.
local_ancestry_truth True local ancestry per sample as source-population tracts.
sim.admixture_demography Build a two-source admixture demography with a post-pulse census.

Generators & model

The ancestry CTMC generator, transition matrices, stationary distribution, and tip emission model.

make_generator_2state Build the 2-state ancestry CTMC generator (CLAUDE.md §2.1).
model.transition_matrix Branch transition probabilities P(t) = expm(Q t) (CLAUDE.md §2.1).
model.stationary_distribution Stationary distribution π of a CTMC generator.
model.tip_emission Soft-clamp emission for a labelled tip (CLAUDE.md §2.2).
model.query_emission Flat / root-frequency emission for an unlabelled query tip (CLAUDE.md §2.2).
model.validate_generator Check that Q is a valid CTMC generator.

Experiment drivers

End-to-end drivers used to produce the validation results (the tspaint.experiments namespace).

experiments.admixture_experiment Simulate admixture, fit hard-clamp EM on the references, paint the queries, score.
experiments.age_sweep Accuracy vs. admixture age — the §9 headline.
experiments.scaling_sweep Runtime + correctness + flicker vs. sample size.
experiments.fragmentation_experiment Fragmentation / tract-length fidelity of hard segmentations vs. the true tracts.
experiments.arg_ensemble_experiment Merge LAI across an ensemble of inferred ARGs vs. a single inferred ARG.
experiments.singer_ensemble_experiment Merge LAI across SINGER posterior ARG samples vs. a single sample vs. truth.
experiments.flicker_vs_true_boundaries Split a sample’s segment-boundary flicker into at-true vs. off-true boundaries.

ARG-posterior ensemble merge

Marginalise ARG uncertainty by averaging per-position paintings across a posterior ensemble (e.g. SINGER samples).

ensemble.merge_posterior_tables Average per-haplotype posteriors across an ensemble of paintings.
ensemble.MergedSegment One span of an ensemble-merged painting.

Lower-level machinery

The pieces underneath the EM: per-root pruning, edge-blocked sufficient statistics, per-branch Van Loan reward integrals, the closed-form M-step, and persistence diagnostics.

pruning.prune_tree Prune every root of a marginal tree and aggregate the results.
pruning.prune_root Up/down sum-product pass for a single root of a marginal tree.
accumulate.accumulate_sufficient_statistics Single E-step sweep over the tree sequence (CLAUDE.md §3.3).
branch_stats.branch_expected_stats Endpoint-conditioned expected dwell times and jump counts on one branch.
em.build_emissions Build the per-sample Felsenstein emission vectors (CLAUDE.md §2.2).
em.m_step_Q Closed-form generator MLE from expected dwell times and jump counts.
em.m_step_pi Closed-form root-frequency MLE: π = S_root / Σ S_root (CLAUDE.md §3.4).
em.m_step_w Per-tip credibility MAP under a Beta(alpha, beta) prior (CLAUDE.md §3.4).
ranked.ranked_tree_sequence Copy of ts with node times replaced by the dense rank of each time.
diagnostics.persistence_summary Summary of internal-node persistence (the go/no-go on the method’s premise).
diagnostics.node_persistence Number of distinct marginal trees in which each (internal) node appears.
diagnostics.edge_span_summary Summary of the tree-sequence edge-span distribution.
